        1 cup & Unsweetened Applesauce\\
        1/2 cup & Oil\\
        1/2 cup & Sugar\\
        1 cup & Raisins\\
        1/2 cup & Chopped Walnuts\\
        1 3/4 cup & Flour\\
        2 tsp & Baking Powder\\
        1 1/2 tsp & Cinnamon\\
        1/2 tsp & Ground Cloves\\
    }
    
    \preparation{%
        \step Preheat the oven.
        \step Combine first six ingredients, and mix well.
        \step Sift flour, baking powder, and spices.  Add to apple mix.
        \step Pour into three greased mini bread pans,
        \step Bake for 30 - 40 minutes.  
        \step Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans.
